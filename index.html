About our company

Photo by @danielnorris

Dizer is not just an application, it's a whole team work. We were inspired by the idea once we realized that there are few applications that can solve the problem with the choice of a great and original place for photography

What are our main features?

Hmm, let is think...

A lot of markers around the world

Dizer is a service where you can find great places for your new photos with the help of maps and markers.

Level up

You can learn something new in editing a photo. Also advertise your page or page of your business.Additionally, we will choose the best photo for months and you have the opportunity to get on the cover of our site or page on facebook.

Earn money and become more popular

For only a few dollars you can buy a subscription to a professional photographer and follow his advice to improve your skill. Or if you are ready to share your knowledge in the art of photography, we invite you to open your personal blog. This is a great opportunity to find like-minded people and earn some money.
